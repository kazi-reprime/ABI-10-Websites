Programs & Tuition

Programs, tuition, and what you'll learn

Five programs across two campuses. Three flexible tracks: Morning, Afternoon, Weekend. NY State Master Barber License at the end.

Choose your program

Every program is state-licensed and exam-prep ready. Tuition is flexible — every plan includes a down payment and a weekly payment schedule.

Flagship
Manhattan · ≈ 17 weeks / 4 months

500-Hour Master Barber Program

$4,600–$5,600

The complete, state-approved path to your New York Master Barber License. Cutting, fading, beard work, straight-razor shaving, sanitation, sterilization, barber history, NY State law, shop management, and full Board Exam prep — written and practical.

Hands-on training in the live student clinic from week one. Choose Morning (Mon–Fri 8AM–2PM), Afternoon (Mon–Fri 2PM–8PM), or Weekend (Sat–Sun 9AM–7PM).

Manhattan · 2-week intensive

50-Hour Barber Refresher

$1,500

A fast, focused refresher for licensed or returning barbers who want to sharpen speed, technique, and confidence on modern fades, beard work, and clipper-over-comb.

Perfect after a career gap, a move to NY, or a license transfer.

Manhattan · Single session

3-Hour Contagious Diseases

Call for pricing

NY State-required infection-control certification — sanitation, sterilization, blood-borne pathogens, OSHA, and compliance practices for the barbershop.

A single session you can complete in an afternoon. Required for all NY barbers.

Bronx · ≈ 18 weeks

540-Hour Master Barber — Bronx

$4,600–$5,600

The full Master Barber certification program at the Westchester Square campus. Same curriculum and exam prep as the Manhattan program, in the Bronx.

Same three flexible schedules. Same instructor-led clinic. Same NY State Master Barber License at the end.

Enrollment

Three steps to your first chair

01

Apply

Submit your information online or call admissions. The whole form takes less than two minutes.

02

Speak with an advisor

An admissions advisor walks you through tuition options, the GI Bill / ACCES-VR process, and the schedule that fits your life.

03

Start training

New cohorts start the first Monday of every month. Complete enrollment, gather your tools list, and step onto the clinic floor.

What you'll need

Documents to enroll

  • Social Security card
  • High School Diploma, GED, or pass the ATB exam at ABI
  • Minimum age 17
  • Proof of residential address
  • Valid photo ID or driver's license
  • $500 down payment (includes $100 registration fee)
Career earnings

What barbers earn

Year 1

Entry-Level Barber

$35,000 – $45,000

Building a client base, refining technique, learning the rhythm of a working shop floor.

Years 2–3

Established Barber

$50,000 – $70,000

Loyal clientele, faster service, increased income, brand starting to travel.

Year 3+

Booth Renter / Shop Owner

$75,000 – $100,000+

Full control of schedule, pricing, and brand. Most graduates aiming for ownership land here within five years.

Earnings figures are estimates only and are not guaranteed. Actual income will vary based on individual effort, hours worked, location, and market conditions.

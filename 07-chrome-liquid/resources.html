Veterans & GI Bill® Benefits

ABI is approved to certify enrollment for veteran education benefits. We accept:

  • Post-9/11 GI Bill® (Chapter 33)
  • VR&E — Vocational Rehabilitation & Employment (Chapter 31)
  • Montgomery GI Bill® (Active Duty & Selected Reserve)
  • DEA — Dependents' Educational Assistance (Chapter 35)

Coverage may pay all or part of your tuition. Talk to admissions about certifying your benefits before your start date.

ACCES-VR

ACCES-VR (Adult Career and Continuing Education Services — Vocational Rehabilitation) is a New York State program that supports individuals with documented disabilities in obtaining and maintaining employment.

  • Coverage may include tuition, tools, and supplies
  • Contact your local ACCES-VR office to begin
  • ABI assists with the referral and certification process

NY State Board of Barbering

  • 500-hour Master Barber program covers both written and practical exam prep
  • Master Barber License required to practice professionally in New York
  • NY State Board info: op.nysed.gov/prof/barber

Tools & Supplies

  • Students purchase their own tools, books, and supplies
  • ABI provides a detailed tools list at registration
  • Buy from ABI directly or from approved third-party suppliers

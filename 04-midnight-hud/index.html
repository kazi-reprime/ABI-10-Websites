Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the program take?
The 500-Hour Master Barber Program can be completed in as little as 4 months, depending on your schedule. The 50-Hour Refresher is a 2-week intensive, and the 3-Hour Contagious Diseases course is a single session.
When do new classes start?
New classes begin the first Monday of every month — so you never have to wait long. Apply, gather your documents, and be ready for the next start date.
What are the entrance requirements?
A Social Security card, a High School Diploma / GED (or pass the ATB exam at ABI), proof of address, a valid photo ID, being at least 17, and a $500 down payment (which includes a $100 registration fee).
How much does the program cost?
The 500-Hour program starts at $4,600 (Afternoon and Weekend plans); the Morning plan is $5,600. The 50-Hour Refresher is $1,500. Flexible weekly payment plans are available for every program.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. Every program includes a weekly payment plan after your down payment. For example, the Afternoon plan is $500 down, then 16 weekly payments of $250 plus a final $100.
What license will I earn?
On completing the 500-Hour Master Barber Program you'll be fully prepared for the New York State Board of Barbering exam — both written and practical — and your Master Barber License.
Do you offer job placement?
Yes. On graduation you'll meet with our Job Placement Office, who help you explore opportunities, connect with employers, and take the next steps in your career.
Do you accept GI Bill® or VA benefits?
Yes. ABI accepts the Post-9/11 GI Bill® and VA education benefits, as well as ACCES-VR vocational rehabilitation funding for those who qualify.
Is ABI licensed by the state?
Yes. American Barber Institute is licensed by the New York State Department of Education and has served New York for over 30 years.
Can I get a haircut at ABI?
Yes — the public is welcome for affordable student haircuts (typically around $3). It's a great way to help trainees reach their required hours while getting a quality cut.
